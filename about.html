About SARUKU

Your trusted partner for quality products and exceptional service since 2010

Our Story

SARUKU was founded with a simple mission: to make online shopping easy, reliable, and enjoyable for everyone.

What started as a small online bookstore in 2010 has grown into one of the most trusted e-commerce platforms, offering millions of products across dozens of categories. Our journey has been driven by our commitment to customer satisfaction and innovation.

Today, we serve customers in over 50 countries, with a team of dedicated professionals working around the clock to ensure the best shopping experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize online shopping by providing:

  • Wide selection of quality products
  • Competitive prices and regular discounts
  • Fast and reliable shipping
  • Exceptional customer service
  • Secure and easy payment options

Our Journey

2010 - Foundation

SARUKU was founded as an online bookstore with just 100 books in our catalog.

2012 - Expansion

Expanded to include electronics and home appliances. Reached 10,000 customers.

2015 - Mobile App

Launched our mobile app, making shopping more convenient for our customers.

2018 - International

Started international shipping to 20 countries. Surpassed 1 million products.

2023 - Today

Serving customers in 50+ countries with 5 million+ products and 500+ employees.
